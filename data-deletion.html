Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how users of Your App Name can request the deletion of their personal data collected by the app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

Steps to Request Data Deletion

  1. Access Your Account Settings: Log in to the app and navigate to Account > Privacy > Data Management (or similar section). Look for a "Delete My Data" or "Request Deletion" button.
  2. Submit a Request: Fill out the form with your full name, email address associated with the app, and a brief reason (optional). Confirm your identity if prompted.
  3. Alternative: Email Us Directly: If the in-app option isn't available, send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Include:
    • Your full name and app username/email.
    • Details of the data you want deleted (e.g., all personal data).
    • A description of your request.
Note: Requests must come from the account holder or an authorized representative. We may require verification (e.g., via a code sent to your email).

Processing Your Request

We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours and process it within 30 days (or sooner, as required by law). This includes:

  • Deleting your personal data from our servers and backups.
  • Notifying any third-party services (if applicable).
  • Stopping further processing of your data.

Confirmation

Once complete, we'll send a confirmation email to the address you provided, detailing what was deleted. If your request is denied (e.g., due to legal obligations), we'll explain why.

For questions, contact us at your-email@example.com. Our full Privacy Policy has more details.
